# AI Job Recommendation Tools — Reference for This System

This document lists popular AI-powered career and job recommendation tools. Use it to compare features, inspire improvements, and understand how similar systems work.

---

## How These AI Tools Work

Most AI job systems use:

- **Resume analysis** — Parse skills, experience, education
- **Skill matching** — Match user profile to job requirements
- **Machine learning models** — Learn from user behavior and job market data
- **Job market data** — Demand, salary, growth trends

They match: **Your skills + experience → Best job recommendations**

---

## Best AI Job Recommendation Tools

### 1. LinkedIn (AI-powered jobs)

- Suggests jobs based on profile, skills, and activity
- AI career advice, resume tips, and job matching
- Search: e.g. "software jobs with remote work"
- Very popular worldwide  
- **AI helps match your skills with jobs automatically**

### 2. Eightfold.ai

- Analyzes your resume
- Suggests best career paths
- Shows skill gaps
- Step-by-step guidance to reach a job  
- **Very useful for students & career changers**

### 3. FuturU

- Recommends careers based on interests
- Uses job market trends
- Shows future demand jobs
- Career fit score  
- **Good for long-term career planning**

### 4. Jobscan

- Matches your CV with job descriptions
- Improves chances of getting selected
- AI checks keywords and skills  
- **Helps you get better job recommendations** (not a job finder directly)

### 5. HiringCafe

- Finds jobs from company websites
- AI filters (no recruiters)
- Real job openings  
- **Alternative to traditional job sites**

### 6. Magnet.me

- Connects with companies using AI
- Recommends jobs based on profile
- Builds long-term career connections  
- **More than job search — builds career network**

### 7. Promilo

- Jobs + internships + learning
- AI suggests career paths for students  
- **Good for beginners and freshers**
